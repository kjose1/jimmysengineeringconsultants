Our Services

Feasibility and Concept Design

Our feasibility and concept design service for electrical power systems involves assessing the practicality and viability of implementing a power system and creating a preliminary conceptual design. This process includes evaluating power requirements, available energy sources, infrastructure, safety considerations, planning requirements and environmental impacts. The aim is to determine if the proposed power system is technically and economically feasible. Concept design focuses on outlining the basic layout, equipment, and technologies required. It provides a foundation for further Front End Engineering Design (FEED) and implementation. Through meticulous analysis, feasibility and concept design service, power systems are designed with efficiency, reliability, and sustainability in mind.

Front End Engineering Design (FEED)

Front End Engineering Design (FEED) service for electrical power systems involves Front End Engineering that follows the feasibility and concept design stages. It encompasses the development of a comprehensive design package that includes technical specifications, equipment selection, system layout, and other crucial details. During FEED, our primary focus will be on refining the concept design, conducting further analysis, and finalizing the engineering layouts and plans. This includes performing load calculations, specifying equipment, preparing electrical drawings, considering safety, environmental considerations and regulatory requirements. Our FEED service would provide a solid foundation for the detailed design phase and implementation of the electrical power system.

Owner's Engineer (OE)

Owners Engineering for electrical power systems involves independent technical and project management support provided to the owner of the power system project. Our owner's engineering service serves to ensure that the project is executed successfully, meeting client's objectives and requirements. It involves activities such as overseeing project planning, design, procurement, construction, and commissioning. As owner's engineer, we provide expertise and guidance in areas such as project management, technical evaluations, vendor selection, quality assurance, and compliance with industry standards and regulations. We act as the owner's representative, helping to minimize risks, optimize project performance, and ensure the successful implementation of the electrical power system.

Design Management

Our Design management service for electrical power systems involves overseeing the entire design process of a power system project, ensuring its efficiency, quality, and successful execution. It encompasses coordinating and guiding the design team, which may include engineers, and other relevant professionals. Design management involves tasks such as setting design objectives, establishing design criteria, monitoring progress, reviewing design deliverables, and ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations. It also includes managing design changes, resolving design conflicts, and maintaining effective communication between different stakeholders involved in the project. Design management plays a crucial role in achieving a well-coordinated and optimized design for the electrical power system.

Battery Energy Storage (BESS) Design

Our BESS design service for electrical power systems involves the planning and configuration of energy storage technologies, primarily batteries, to store and release electricity on-demand. The design process includes determining the required capacity, energy storage duration, and power output capabilities of the BESS. Factors such as load profiles, renewable energy integration, grid stability, and economic considerations are taken into account. Additionally, BESS design involves selecting the appropriate battery chemistry, sizing the system, designing the power electronics and control systems, and ensuring compatibility with the existing power infrastructure. The goal of BESS design is to enhance grid stability, improve renewable energy integration, support peak shaving, and enable effective energy management.

Distributed Generation Design

Our Distributed Generation design service offers system integration from small-scale power sources located near the point of consumption, in contrast to traditional centralized power generation. It involves the deployment of various energy sources such as solar panels, wind turbines, microturbines, fuel cells, and small-scale generators. DG systems can be installed in residential, commercial, or industrial settings to provide on-site power generation and reduce reliance on the grid. The key benefits of distributed generation include increased energy efficiency, reduced transmission losses, improved grid resilience, and the potential for renewable energy integration. DG plays a significant role in transitioning towards a more decentralized and sustainable electrical power system with a lower carbon footprint.
